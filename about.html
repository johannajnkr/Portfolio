Johanna Janker is a photographer currently based in Munich, Germany.

Born in 2002, she specializes in doing documentary, portrait and fashion work.

Shooting on film and enjoying the workflow of everything that comes with doing it the analog way,

has become a way for her to fully indulge in the process of taking photos.
